Computer Science Undergraduate Center

Aspiring computer scientists are of just as great importance to the community
as are graduate computer scientists. Equipped with the appropriate resources and
drive towards excellence, undergrads can achieve lengths of great heights.
As the computer science community, we intend to nurture and cultivate our undergraduate
computer scientists into beings of utmost excellence.

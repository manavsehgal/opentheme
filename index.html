Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science, part of the Faculty of Science and Engineering, offers a range of specialised undergraduate and postgraduate degree courses.

Vision

To be a leading academic centre of excellence in Africa and the world, through advancement and promotion of scholarship, research and innovation in the field of computing.

Mission

To advance the intellectual and human resource capacity of the nation and the international community in the field of computing by offering quality academic programmes that are bench-marked against international standards.
To produce computer scientists who are professionally competent, responsible and innovative in solving real-world problems.
To promote scholarship through research that addresses national and societal needs, and advances scientific knowledge.

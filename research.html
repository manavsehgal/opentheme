Research Excellence

Computer Science department is engaged and committed to incept projects of
institutional and national importance in the themes of Research, Innovation and
Technology Transfer. These projects involve local, regional and international
collaborations and networks and building strategic partnerships.
The overarching aims of the projects are to:

  • Strengthen the Department of Computer Science and University's research
    capacity- this to provide a concrete realization of the university research
    strategy and vision;
  • Stimulate multidisciplinary intra and inter faculty research,
  • Enhance local, regional and international collaborations and to provide
    outreach and visibility.
  • Develop capacity in Innovation, Technology Transfer and Intellectual
    Property
  • Stimulation of grassroots innovation and interfacing with Technology
    Transfer Offices and Incubators.

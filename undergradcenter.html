Available Opportunities For Certification

Whilst pursuing a degree, many undergradutes should also consider opportunities for program certification while still an undergradute. For such opportunities usually present themselves for less costs while still a student. In addition, a certified computer science graduate has an advantage over an un-certified graduate in the world of employment.

Such opportunities come in the form of:
  • CISCO
  • SAP
  • ORACLE

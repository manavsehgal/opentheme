Computer Science Undergraduate Center

Aspiring computer scientists are just as great of an importance to the community as are graduate computer scientists. Equipped with the appropriate resources and drive towards excellence, undergrads can achieve lengths of great heights.
As the computer science community, we intend to nurture and cultivate our undergraduate computer scientists into beings of utmost excellence.

Alas, we intend to undergo the nurturing process by ensuring provision of resources, activities and presentation of opportunities within the Univeristy of Botswana community as well as within the external environment that will aid our aspiring computer scientists in their pursuit of excellence.

The Department of Computer Science offers the following undergraduate programmes:

Single Major programmes leading to the award of:

  1. B.Sc. (Computer Science)
  2. B.Sc. (Computing with Finance)
  3. BIS. (Computer Information Systems)
  4. B.Sc. (Information Technology)

Combined Major/Minor programmes leading to the award of:
  1. B.Sc. (Other subject Major/ Computer Science Minor)

Available Opportunities For Certification

Whilst pursuing a degree, many undergradutes should also consider opportunities for program certification while still an undergradute. For such opportunities usually present themselves for less costs while still a student. In addition, a certified computer science graduate has an advantage over an un-certified graduate in the world of employment.
